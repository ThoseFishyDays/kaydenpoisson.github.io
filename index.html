About Me


I've loved art and storytelling for as long as I can remember. As a kid, I'd fill notebooks with doodles and make up stories about superheroes and mythical creatures. I've been homeschooled for a large portion of my life, but for the times I have gone, I've noticed it was extremely hard for me to pay attention. Instead, I'd be covering my homework in drawings and patterns.


Like a lot of kids, I cycled through dream jobs, archaeologist, astronaut, zookeeper. But art was the one thing that always stuck. No matter what phase I was in, I never got tired of creating. Eventually, I gave in and decided to pursue what I love and what I will love doing in the future, as my career.


Over the years, I've experimented with all kinds of styles, from realism to anime. But the one that I was drawn to is simple, a bit cartoonish, and (usually) cute. It's the style I keep coming back to, no matter which other styles I try. Also, somewhat recently, I have really enjoyed making products, seeing my designs on shirts or mugs gives me a feeling of achievement. I have so many ideas for the future pursuing my product design career, and I'm excited to get started!
