About Me


At a young age, I fell in love with art and stories. I would make up stories about superheroes and fantasy creatures. I struggled through school most of my life, because I couldn't pay attention and would always doodle on my homework sheets.


I wasn't sure what I wanted to be growing up, an archeologist, an astronaut, the classics. But I figured I'll always love art, and there will never be a time where I get tired of it. So, why not put it to use and make it my career?


I've been through many different styles throughout my life, sometimes I'd try realism, sometimes I'd try anime. But, there was always one style that I could consitantly draw, and I'm happy to say I'd call it my own. Simple, a little cartoonish, most of the time cute.
